History

Ayala Corporation is one of the oldest and largest conglomerates in the Philippines, with a history dating back to 1834. It was founded by Don Domingo Roxas and his brother-in-law, Don Jose Maria Ayala, initially focusing on the retail and real estate sectors. Over the years, Ayala expanded its business portfolio, venturing into banking, telecommunications, water infrastructure, energy, and various other industries. Through strategic leadership and innovation, Ayala has become a key player in shaping the Philippine economy, with a commitment to sustainable development and corporate social responsibility. Today, Ayala continues to be a leading business group in the country, renowned for its diversified holdings and enduring legacy.
